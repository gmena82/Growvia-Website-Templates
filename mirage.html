Frequently asked questions

Do I own the content I generate?

Yes. You have full commercial rights to all videos created in Mirage Studio, including the AI actors you generate. There are no ongoing licensing fees or usage restrictions—the content is yours to use, publish, and scale across any platform or campaign. Read more in our Terms and Conditions.

Can I create a digital version of myself or of another real person?

As long as you have their approval, Mirage Studio can turn real people into reusable digital actors. You can use reference images or footage to generate additional footage beyond what exists today. So for example, you could showcase founders, brand ambassadors or build your own thought leadership brand. It’s perfect for consistent, on-brand content.

What makes Mirage Studio different from other AI avatar tools?

Unlike many AI avatar tools that rely on pre-rendered templates or basic lip-syncing over existing footage, Mirage is the first of its kind foundation model that generates each actor and performance from scratch. That includes photorealistic actors, head movement, eye contact, hand gestures, and emotional expression—all tightly synced to the voice track. The result is a fully synthetic, production-ready video that feels human, not robotic.

Can I control the look and feel of the AI actors?

Yes, Mirage gives you full creative control over your AI actor’s appearance. You can adjust their face, hairstyle, skin tone, outfit, voice, background, and more. Whether you’re creating a polished spokesperson, a casual creator-style persona, or anything in between, the system is designed to match your brand and audience needs. Learn more tips on how to generate an AI actor from a prompt here.

Can I collaborate with my team in Mirage Studio?

Mirage Studio supports team collaboration. You can invite team members, assign roles and permissions, share credits, and work together on projects from a shared workspace.

What’s the difference between Mirage Studio and Captions?

Mirage Studio is specifically built for marketers and creative teams. It’s intended to help teams create ads with AI and can scale asset libraries quickly. Mirage Studio removes typical barriers for producing new footage, so you have more raw video fast. Captions is built for smaller scale operations where you don’t have an editor and need to create the entire video directly in the app.

Can I use Mirage Studio to create AI-generated ads for social platforms?

Yes, you can. When you create ads with Mirage Studio’s AI solutions, you can move faster and scale ads quicker than using traditional video production processes or other video platforms. Create as many ad variations as you need, then rapidly iterate by changing details like hooks, your avatars’ clothing or other details. It’s helpful for marketing teams and agencies who want to run high-quality ads at scale.
